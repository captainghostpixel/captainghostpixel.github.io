Idea Planning   

My original idea was to do a website about the free-to-play game, Genshin Impact. However, when it came to creating my own media for the site I knew it would be harder to achieve. I inevitably ended up deciding to do a webite for the television show 'Ghosts' on BBC One. I decided this because it is a show I am as equally interested in as Genshin Impact and as the show is set in England I would be able to replicate the scenery and tone of 'Button House' (the setting) with photos of nature and trinkets.
